❓ How Blockchain Protects You

Your medicine's journey — immutably recorded

🏭

1. Manufacturing

The drug batch is registered by a licensed manufacturer with a unique Batch ID, composition, and expiry date — permanently on the blockchain.

🚚

2. Distribution

Every handoff through the supply chain is recorded immutably. Any tampering breaks the provenance chain and triggers alerts.

💊

3. Pharmacy

Pharmacists verify authenticity before dispensing. Recalled or expired drugs are automatically blocked by the smart contract.

🔍

4. You Verify

You can verify your medicine's entire journey from factory to your hands using the Batch ID on the packaging — in seconds.
